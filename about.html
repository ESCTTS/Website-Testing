About Us

      

If you live in Redhill, Reigate or Merstham or nearby villages such as Earlswood, Gatton or Salfords — the Redhill History Centre is for you.

      

We have old maps, census records, parish documents, and tons of local history material. We support everyone — from school kids to lifelong learners — and help trace family stories, homes, and historical mysteries.
