Visit Us

      

You can find us on the first floor of Redhill Library at the Harlequin Centre. Visit anytime during library hours — no booking needed.

      

We offer free access to Ancestry, Find My Past, and the British Newspaper Archive. Our volunteer historians are available on the 1st and 3rd Saturdays each month, 2pm–4pm, and by arrangement.
